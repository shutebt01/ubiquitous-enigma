Basic maths stuff

Syntax

If you haven't seen the page on our syntax (or if it doesn't exist) then heres a quick rundown: 

  • ^
    or 
    **
    - Means To the power of - Example: 
    x^2
  • cos(x)
    , 
    sin(x)
    etc. - Trig functions. - Example: 
    cos(x)/sin(x)
  • sqrt(x)
    , 
    cbrt(x)
    and 
    root(x,n)
    - Square root x, Cube Root x, or n Root x - Example: 
    root(x^2, 5)
